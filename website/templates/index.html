education and its importance

"Education is the key to unlock the golden door of freedom"
- George Washington Carver

Golden door of freedom is about the stability, financial security, self dependency and economic growth.

These heavy words are in actual very important. One must have stability in their life so the person will succeed in learning the lessons of management. Education provides financial security which ensures that you are capable enough to fulfill your needs. Especially in today's world, being independent is the formost and prime thing and self dependency is acquired by being educated and it is something that no one can take away from you. Thus an educated society contributes in building the economic growth of one's and also on a national level.
